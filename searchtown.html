Titan: Surface

Story



You head towards the town, following a dirt track leading in.

Entering, you find the area dotted in small huts and houses. Further in, small shops and stalls engulf you in scents and smoke, with vibrant colours all around.

You manage to find a repairman who knows a small amount about engine repairs. Leading him back to your ship, toolbox in hand, you tell him what happened to the ship, explaining the engine issues.

He begins repairing the engine, repurposing scrap metal brought along. The man is able to fix the engine, bringing it roaring back to life.

You leave Titan.
